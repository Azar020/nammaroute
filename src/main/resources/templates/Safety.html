🛡️ General Travel Safety Tips

  • Always check schedules on official sources or trusted apps.
  • Avoid deserted areas while waiting for transport, especially at night.
  • Keep valuables hidden and use bags with secure zippers.
  • Use only authorized autos/taxis — check license numbers.
  • Inform a family member or friend of your travel plans.

📞 Emergency Contacts

  • Emergency (India): 112
  • Police: 100
  • Ambulance: 108
  • Railway Helpline: 139
  • Chennai Metro: 9445030516

👩‍🦰 Women's Travel Safety

  • Install safety apps like Kavalan SOS or SHE Teams.
  • Trust your instincts — avoid uncomfortable situations.
  • Use mobile apps to share your live location with friends.

📱 Recommended Apps

  • Kavalan SOS: Tamil Nadu Police safety app
  • CMRL Metro App: For metro timings and alerts
  • Google Maps: Real-time location sharing
