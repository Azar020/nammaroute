🚀 Our Mission

At NammaRoute, we empower commuters with smart, safe, and efficient route planning. Whether you're catching a metro, hopping on a bus, or using local trains, our platform ensures the best route in terms of time, safety, and cost.

💡 Why Choose NammaRoute?

  • 📍 Multi-modal transport integration (Metro, Train, Bus)
  • 📊 Real-time suggestions based on cost, time, and safety
  • 🛡️ Route safety indicators and emergency contact info
  • 📱 Mobile-friendly and easy-to-use interface

🌐 Vision for the Future

NammaRoute aims to be the go-to urban navigation platform for every Indian city, combining open data, citizen feedback, and cutting-edge AI to make commuting smarter and safer.
